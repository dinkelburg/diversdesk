The heart of your daily operations has a brand-new look and feel, designed to reduce clicks and keep you organized.

## Architecture

### Left Panel
In the left panel you'll find the participants or guests of this booking, as well as the booking share options.

### Right Panel
In the right panel, you'll find the bill and payment information. 
The top section prominently displays the outstanding balance and a payment ledger with payment history 
You also got the option to generate invoices and with this lock the booking.

### Middle content section
The middle section will hold a summary card in the top and all activities listed below. If there are items from different segments added to this booking, there will be navigation buttons displayed for each segment.

**🎯 Context-Specific Menus**
The individual activity cards now feature an **Ellipsis (⋮)** menu. This allows you to perform specific actions—like editing, cancelling, or reassigning—directly on an individual activity or item without leaving your current view.

**📊 Enhanced Visibility**
* **Taxes & Fees** Tax rates per set per Activity Category or and show now automomatically in the bill & invoice if set.
* **Cleaner Interface:** Easier insight in guest details and trip information.
