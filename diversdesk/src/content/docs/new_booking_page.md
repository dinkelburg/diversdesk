## Architecture

### Left Panel
The left panel displays the participants or guests for the booking, along with booking share options.

### Right Panel
In the right panel, you’ll find billing and payment information. The top section prominently displays the outstanding balance and a payment ledger with full history. <br>
As before, you can generate invoices here that will automatically lock the booking for consistency.

### Middle content section
The middle section features a summary card at the top with all activities listed below. If items from different segments are added to the booking, navigation buttons for each segment will appear automatically.

## Actions

#### Cancel a Booking

Cancelling a booking can be done through the +Manage button at the top-right of your booking page.

:::tip
  Using the Cancel Booking option in the +Manage button will cancel the entire booking. To remove individual activities, simply click on the specific item card. This opens a side panel where you can remove that activity without affecting the rest of the booking.
:::
