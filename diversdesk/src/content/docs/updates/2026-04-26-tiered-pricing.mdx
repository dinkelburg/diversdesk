Release Date: April 26, 2026

## Tiered Pricing Is Here

We’ve added a new **Tiered Pricing** tab on the pricing page, right next to the existing fixed price setup.

This makes it easier to build packages where the number of dives changes the final price automatically, so you can offer flexible pricing without having to work around it manually.

## What Is New

* **New pricing tab:** Tiered pricing now lives on the pricing page as an additional tab alongside fixed prices.
* **Flexible package pricing:** Ideal for packages where dives can be added and the price updates automatically.
* **Automatic tier calculation:** Pricing adjusts based on the selected tier, helping you keep your offers clear and consistent.

---

## How It Works

Tiered pricing supports both **volume** and **graduated** pricing setups.

That means you can define how the price should behave as more dives are added, and Diversdesk will calculate the correct price for the package automatically.

In practice, this gives you a faster way to set up bundled offers, multi-dive packages, and other pricing structures that don’t fit a single fixed price.

---

## Why It Helps

* **Less manual work:** No need to keep adjusting package prices by hand.
* **Clearer offers:** Customers can be shown a pricing structure that matches the package they are booking.
* **More flexibility:** Great for dive bundles, training packs, and other package-based products.
