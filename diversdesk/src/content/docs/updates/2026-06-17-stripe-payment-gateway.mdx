Release Date: June 17, 2026

## Stripe Payments Are Now Integrated

You can now use **Stripe** as a payment gateway in Diversdesk.

Stripe is available for customers in **Stripe-supported regions**, giving more dive centers access to reliable online card payments.

With this release, we expand our payment gateway integrations and support more businesses with flexible payment options.

## Updates in This Release

* **Stripe payment gateway integration:** connect Stripe and accept online card payments.
* **Available in Stripe-supported regions:** use Stripe where regional support is available.
* **Smoother guest checkout experience:** guests complete payments in a familiar and trusted flow.
* **More dependable payments:** transactions are handled more consistently from checkout to confirmation.

---

### Stripe Payment Gateway Integration

You can now connect Stripe and process online card payments directly through Diversdesk.

Once set up, guests pay through a flow they already recognize, which helps build trust and reduces hesitation at checkout.

This integration is available for customers operating in Stripe-supported regions.

---

### Smoother Guest Checkout Experience

Guests now complete payment in a cleaner, more familiar checkout experience. This reduces friction during booking and can improve completion rates, especially for first-time customers.

The flow is designed to feel straightforward on both desktop and mobile.

---

### More Reliable Payment Processing

With Stripe integrated, payment handling is more consistent and predictable. Payment outcomes are clearer, so your team can follow each transaction with more confidence.

In day-to-day use, this means fewer manual follow-ups and a more dependable payment journey for both your team and your guests.
