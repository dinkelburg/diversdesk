<p>
    As a resort owner, you know the "spreadsheet shuffle" all too well. A guest books a 5-day dive package, and suddenly you’re jumping between tabs to check room availability, manually blocking out dates, and hoping nothing gets double-booked in the process.
</p>
<p>
    At Diversdesk, we believe managing your resort should be as calm as a safety stop. That’s why we are thrilled to unveil our brand-new <strong>Accommodation Module</strong>. The final piece of the puzzle for full-circle resort management.
</p>

<h2 id="why-this-changes-everything" class="section-heading">Why This Changes Everything for Your Resort</h2>
<p>
    Most software handles either the dive or the stay. Diversdesk now does both, natively. No more fragmented guest profiles, disconnected billing, or "manual sync" errors.
</p>

<ul style="list-style-type: disc; margin-left: 20px; margin-top: 8px;">
    <li><strong>True "Dive & Stay" Integration:</strong> When a guest books a package, the system doesn’t just record the dives, it automatically locks in the room. Real-time availability means you can sell with confidence.</li>
    <li><strong>A PMS Built for Divers:</strong> View your entire resort at a glance. Manage room assignments, bed configurations, and guest preferences without leaving the platform.</li>
    <li><strong>The "One-Bill" Experience:</strong> Your guests receive a single, professional summary that covers their room, dives, equipment rentals, and even airport transfers.</li>
    <li><strong>Modular & Flexible:</strong> We separate "Accommodation Details" from "Travel Details," letting you track flight info without cluttering your room management.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-8 mb-4">Sell More, Manage Less: The Unified Webshop & Channel Manager</h3>
<p class="mb-4">The new Diversdesk Webshop isn't just a booking tool, it’s a storefront that lives on your website and works while you sleep. We’ve bridged the gap between your online sales and your daily operations.</p>

<ul style="list-style-type: disc; margin-left: 20px; margin-top: 8px;">
    <li><strong>The Single Source of Truth:</strong> Whether a guest books directly through your website or through an external OTA, the Channel Manager keeps everything in sync. When a room is sold, your inventory updates everywhere instantly. No more double bookings or manual updates.</li>
    <li><strong>The "Unified Cart" Experience:</strong> Give your guests the power to build their own holiday. In one single checkout flow, they can book their accommodation, add a Nitrox package, sign up for a PADI course, and even buy a resort t-shirt from your retail shop.</li>
    <li><strong>Seamless Website Integration:</strong> Our webshop is clean, fast, and designed to match your brand. It removes the friction of "Request a Quote" emails, moving guests directly from "interested" to "confirmed."</li>
</ul>


<h2 id="dynamic-pricing" class="section-heading">The Power of Dynamic Pricing & Smart Rules</h2>
<p>
    A unified system is only as good as its ability to handle complex pricing. Our new module introduces <strong>Advanced Price Rules</strong> to handle the heavy lifting for you:
</p>

<ul style="list-style-type: disc; margin-left: 20px; margin-top: 8px;">
    <li><strong>Automated High-Season Pricing:</strong> Set your date ranges once. Whether it’s Christmas, New Year, or peak diving season, Diversdesk automatically adjusts your rates so you never leave money on the table.</li>
    <li><strong>Flexible Occupancy Logic:</strong> Managing solo travelers? You can now set <strong>automated single-person occupancy charges</strong>. The system calculates the supplement the moment the guest is added. No manual math required.</li>
    <li><strong>Tiered Pricing:</strong> Easily scale your rates based on room types or package inclusions, ensuring your premium offerings are always priced accurately.</li>
</ul>
